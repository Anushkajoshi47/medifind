About MediFind

Built to make healthcare discovery effortless

Our Mission

MediFind was built to solve a real problem — when you move to a new city, finding the right specialist, understanding their schedules, and knowing which hospitals they work at is a fragmented, frustrating experience.

We aggregate verified doctor and hospital data to give you a complete, transparent picture before you ever pick up the phone.

What Makes Us Different

  • ✅ Real visiting schedules, not just contact information
  • ✅ Bidirectional mapping — see a hospital's full team or a doctor's full hospital list
  • ✅ Direct call integration — no middlemen
  • ✅ Verified, curated data across 12 major Indian hospitals
